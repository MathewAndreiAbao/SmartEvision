# Smart E-VISION: User Handbook
**Official Guide for Teachers & Supervisors**

## 1. For Teachers
### 1.1 Uploading Documents
- Navigate to **Upload** in the sidebar.
- Select your **Teaching Load** (Subject & Grade).
- Drop your file (DLL, ISP, or ISR).
- The system will automatically detect the **Week Number** and **Subject** using AI.
- Click **Start Upload Pipeline**.

### 1.2 Accessing Your Archive
- View your submission history on the **Dashboard**.
- Navigate to **Archive** to see a Google Drive-style folder structure of your work.
- Download verified copies of your documents anytime.

## 2. For School Heads & Master Teachers
### 2.1 Oversight
- Use the **Analytics** and **Monitoring** tabs to track teacher compliance within your school.
- View the **Compliance Heatmap** to identify missing submissions per week.

## 3. For District Supervisors
### 3.1 District Management
- Manage **Academic Deadlines** in the **Calendar** tab.
- Generate **Official Reports** (PDF/CSV) from the Dashboard.
- Use the **QR Scanner** on your mobile device to verify the authenticity of printed documents.

## 4. Offline Functionality
- If you lose internet connection, you can still upload files.
- They will be queued in the **Offline Sync Queue**.
- The system will automatically sync them once you are back online.
